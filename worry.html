WHY WORRY ABOUT POLLUTION?

Pollution should be a concern because it damages the environment and disrupts natural ecosystems. It can also affect the quality of the air, water, and soil that people depend on daily. If pollution continues to grow, it can lead to long-term problems for both nature and human society.
